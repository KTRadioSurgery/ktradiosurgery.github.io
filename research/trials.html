General Information

Most trials open at Rush University Medical Center for CNS tumors are for patients 18 years of age and older, though there may be exceptions. Trials will also generally require patients to be healthy enough to undergo the proposed treatment being examined in the trial. Details about trial eligibility can be discussed with your Rush provider, though some information is also linked to here.
